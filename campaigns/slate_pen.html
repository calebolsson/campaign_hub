Slate Penitentiary

Setting and Introduction

Somewhere in our star system in an undisclosed location is the largest supermax known to humanity - Slate Penitentiary. It is known for housing the most dangerous of criminals, and if your sentence takes you to Slate you are there for life, unless you get the letter inviting you to what the inmates have jokingly named the 'Clean Slate' program. Nobody knows what really happens to the participants, but the offer states that you would be released with a full pardon, if you survive. When the letter slips between the bars of your cell one day, you think of all the hopes and dreams you left behind when you first came here. You would give anything to get it all back...

~ Slate Penitentiary ~

Current Player Characters

Beth

After coming of age, Beth decided she needed to go off and do her own thing, which lead her to a group of like-minded souls in the Selesnya Conclave. Beth is a little more quiet and reserved, especially having spent the past few years hanging out with hippies. Despite being slow on the social uptake, she is quite agile, and surprisingly witty. To the dismay of her new friends, Beth was mistakenly caught up in a mass-arrest related to her past (since her name was still associated with the real perpetrators). Unable to prove her innocence, she now resides in Slate Penitentiary.

Caed

Caed is a creature of emotion and instinct. On paper, he is a reckless miscreant who has a penchant for mischief and crime. His latest crime has landed him in solitary confinement at Slate Penitentiary. In person? Caed is the most bubbly and enthusiastic person you have ever met. He will make you feel like you're the most important person in the world, until he slices off your hand to keep it as a souvenir. He has a terrible relationship with the Senate. The Azorious have tried to put him through rehab, mental institutions, therapy, but all of those he talks to seem to doubt their reasons for having him there, until he's back. He carries a strong dislike and grudge for anyone tied to the senate, or has any vague connection to them, but at least he's all smiles until then.
